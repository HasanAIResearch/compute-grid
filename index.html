Power and interconnection are the gating layer.If AI demand keeps rising, the scarce asset becomes fast access to firm power and grid capacity.

Memory, packaging, and networking decide cluster scale.GPUs are visible, but HBM, CoWoS-like packaging, switching, optics, and connectors decide how much compute can be deployed.

Institutional lens

Delivery lens for the AI buildout

The useful institutional framing belongs here: track what is announced, what is buildable, what can be energized, and what can turn into revenue.

Capacity mathStart with AI demand, then force it through power, interconnection, equipment, cooling, land, labor, and commissioning gates.
Delivery proofSeparate press releases from permitted sites, equipment orders, construction progress, and energized megawatts.
Bottleneck rotationIf GPUs loosen but HBM, optics, switchgear, cooling, or EPC capacity remains tight, the winners rotate down the stack.
Exposure purityPrefer direct bottleneck suppliers when the theme is hot; treat diversified names as lower-purity read-throughs unless AI backlog is visible.
